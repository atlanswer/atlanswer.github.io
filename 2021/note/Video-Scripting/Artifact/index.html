KNLMeansCL

Banding

De-banding

  • De-band to a degree that nearly every scene in the sample files is banding-free while keeping de-banding strength weak
  • Increase the de-banding strength by 30~50%

f3kdb

Examine chroma banding

Enhance: (x - 32768) * 3 + 32768

Aliasing

Intra-field deinterlacer

Remember to adjust YUV alignment.

Experiment parameter

and a contra-sharpen

For anti-aliasing

  • eedi2: mthresh=10, lthresh=20, vthresh=20, maxd=24, nt=50
  • eedi3: alpha=0.5, beta=0.2, gamma=20.0, nrad=3, mdis=30
  • nnedi3/znedi3: nsize=3, nns=1, qual=2

eedi3: best for edge linking.

nnedi3 is too sharp and not suitable for anti-aliasing.

SangNom

Strong anti-aliasing, but it's also a deinterlacer.

To prevent artifact, widen the image to gap vertical lines.

or combined with eedi2
