Nature Formatting Guide

Reference: Nature for Authors.

Nature Articles

Articles are original reports whose conclusions represent a substantial advance in understanding of an important problem and have immediate, far-reaching implications. In print, they do not normally exceed 5 pages of Nature. Articles start with a fully referenced summary paragraph, ideally of no more than 200 words, which is separate form the main text and avoids numbers, abbreviations, acronyms or measurements unless essential.
