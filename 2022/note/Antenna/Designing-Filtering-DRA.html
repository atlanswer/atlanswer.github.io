Leveraging the advantages of DRA

  • High efficiency
  • Low cost
  • High degree of design flexibility

Basic Filtering DRA Design

  • Slots: can be used to tune the passband and radiation nulls of the filtering antenna
    • SLot I
      • can improve passband matching and stopband supression
    • Slot II
      • resonates in its higher order mode
    • Slot III
      • excites the fundamental \(\rm{TE}^y_{1\delta 1}\) DRA mode
  • Feedline: divided into three parts for matching the antenna
